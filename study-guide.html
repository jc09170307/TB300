Movements 1–4

🧘 Opening Position · 開始 · Before EVERY session

Feet together → step left to shoulder width. Center weight. Hands to lower dantian (one on top of other). Close eyes. Breathe naturally ~30 seconds to calm body and mind.
1

Holding Up the Heavens

雙手托天理三焦
Shuāng Shǒu Tuō Tiān Lǐ Sān Jiāo
Release hands from dantian, relax arms. Clasp hands palms up. Inhale — lift to chest, rotate outward, push to sky. Exhale — release, lower each side palms down, return to dantian. 6 cycles.
↑ Inhale: lift to chest↓ Exhale: push sky, lower to dantian🫁 Triple Warmer · SpineMetal
2

Draw the Bow to Shoot the Eagle

左右開弓似射雕
Zuǒ Yòu Kāi Gōng Sì Shè Diāo
Cross left forearm in front of right at chest. Step left foot 2× shoulder width, toes forward. Inhale — horse stance; pull right fist to shoulder, push left Southern Palm* to left; follow with eyes. Exhale — step back, lower to dantian. Alternate. 6 cycles. *Southern Palm: index extended, other fingers curl inward.
↑ Inhale: horse stance + draw bow↓ Exhale: step back🫁 Lung + Large IntestineMetal
3

Separating Heaven and Earth

調理脾胃須單臂
Tiáo Lǐ Pí Wèi Xū Dān Bì
Feet shoulder width, hands at lower dantian (palms up). Inhale — raise both to chest, palms up; rotate left palm outward. Exhale — push left to sky, right to ground simultaneously. Inhale — lower left, raise right to chest. Exhale — right up, left down. 6 cycles.
↑ Inhale: both to chest↓ Exhale: separate sky/earth🫁 Spleen + StomachEarth
4

Wise Owl Gazes Backwards

五勞七傷向後瞧
Wǔ Láo Qī Shāng Xiàng Hòu Qiáo
Arms at sides, palms down. Inhale — rotate both arms from shoulder, palms outward, thumbs pointing backward. Simultaneously rotate head left, look as far left as possible (turn eyes too). Exhale — return head and arms to start. Repeat right. 6 cycles alternating.
↑ Inhale: rotate arms + turn head↓ Exhale: return to center🫁 Heart + Cervical vesselsFire

Movements 5–8
5

Sway the Head and Shake the Tail

搖頭擺尾去心火
Yáo Tóu Bǎi Wěi Qù Xīn Huǒ
Step left, horse stance, tall, toes forward. Hands on inner thighs near knees. Inhale — rotate torso in full circle (right → left). Circle again, then Exhale — twist left, look behind you, lean over left thigh, right leg extends. Inhale — swing back, horse stance. Second cycle on right side. 6 total cycles. Return to standing.
↑ Inhale: circle torso↓ Exhale: twist, look behind, lean🫁 Heart · Reduces Heart Fire (君火)Fire
6

Hold the Feet to Strengthen the Kidneys

兩手把住足根搖
Liǎng Shǒu Bǎ Zhù Zú Gēn Yáo
Clasp hands at dantian. Inhale — raise to chest. Rotate palms forward, push front, Exhale. Inhale — raise overhead. Release, palms down, pull to chest, Exhale. Inhale — pull hands to back at solar plexus. Exhale — slide hands down entire back, buttocks, hamstrings, outside of feet; extend arms forward. Inhale — lift torso to standing. 6 cycles.
↑ Inhale: raise / lift torso to stand↓ Exhale: slide hands full length down🫁 Kidney + Urinary BladderWater
7

Clench the Fists and Gaze Fiercely

攥拳怒目增氣力
Zuàn Quán Nù Mù Zēng Qì Lì
Step left, horse stance, tall, toes forward. Two fists at sides, palms up (thumbs on top). Exhale — punch left forward, eyes wide open fiercely. Open hand, rotate palm forward (thumb down). Twist fingers inward to fist, Inhale. Exhale — pull left fist back while punching right forward. Alternate. 6 cycles. Return to shoulder width.
↑ Inhale: pull fist back↓ Exhale: punch + fierce eyes wide open🫁 Liver + GallbladderWood
8

Bouncing the Feet and Vibrating the Back

背后七顛百病消
Bèi Hòu Qī Diān Bǎi Bìng Xiāo
Inhale — raise arms to shoulder height, palms down. Simultaneously lift heels, balance on balls of feet. Exhale with extra force — heels drop to floor, arms drop beside you. Keep movement loose and natural. 6 cycles.
↑ Inhale: raise arms + heels together↓ Exhale (forceful): heels + arms drop🫁 All organs · Settles QiWater/All

🌿 Closing Position · 結束 · After EVERY session

Inhale — raise arms to each side, palms up, until just over head. Exhale — lower palms down to lower dantian. Repeat 3 cycles total. Finish with hands on dantian, eyes closed, breathe naturally ≥30 seconds. Open eyes, relax arms, return left foot to right.

TCM Theory
Key TCM Terms · 主要術語
氣
Qi (Chi)
Vital energy/life force flowing through meridians. The fundamental substance of TCM. Qigong cultivates smooth, abundant Qi flow.
丹田
Dantian
Energy center ~2 inches below navel. Home base for all Ba Duan Jin — hands return here between every movement.
精
Jing
Essence — one of the Three Treasures. Stored in kidneys. Related to reproductive energy and longevity. Strengthened by Movement #6.
神
Shen
Spirit/Mind — one of the Three Treasures. Housed in Heart. Governs consciousness and emotions. Calmed through daily Qigong practice.
三焦
San Jiao
Triple Warmer/Burner — governs water metabolism and thermal regulation. Targeted by Movement #1.
經絡
Meridians
Pathways through which Qi flows. 12 primary meridians correspond to organ systems. Qigong promotes smooth flow through all.
陰陽
Yin & Yang
Opposing yet complementary forces. Yin: cool, dark, passive. Yang: warm, light, active. Balance = health.
衛氣
Wei Qi
Defensive Qi on body surface. Protects against external pathogens. Strengthened by immune Qigong (Week 8).
君火
Heart Fire
Excess Fire in Heart causing restlessness, insomnia, anxiety. Specifically reduced by Movement #5.
易筋經
Yi Jin Jing
Muscle/Tendon Changing Classic — more advanced Qigong introduced Week 9. Strengthens tendons and muscles.

Three Treasures · 三寶

Jing 精 · Qi 氣 · Shen 神Essence + Energy + Spirit — the three substances Qigong cultivates and preserves.

CLO 3 Adaptation

CLO 3: Adapt and teach Qi Gong to patients with movement disabilities

Always preserve: breath coordination, mental focus, and the essential organ-targeting action of each movement. Seated and modified-standing versions are both fully acceptable.
#MovementConditionModification
1Holding Up HeavensShoulder injuryReduce arm raise; clasp hands at chest only; skip overhead push
1Holding Up HeavensBalance / fall riskFully seated — same breath and hand movements apply
2Draw the BowKnee / hip arthritisNo horse stance — stand tall; perform all arm/shoulder movements upright
2Draw the BowWheelchair userUpper body only; no stepping; rotate torso in chair
3Separating Heaven & EarthShoulder injuryStop at shoulder level; do not force overhead reach
3Separating Heaven & EarthLow BP / dizzinessSlow movement; pause at chest; sit down if dizzy
4Wise OwlCervical disc herniationLimit head rotation to comfortable range; never force; arm-only if needed
4Wise OwlVertigo / BPPVAvoid or greatly reduce head rotation; arm external rotation only
5Sway Head & TailKnee injuryNo horse stance — stand tall; torso rotation standing
5Sway Head & TailLumbar disc herniationGreatly reduce twist range; never aggressively lean over thigh
6Hold Feet / KidneysLumbar disc diseaseLimit forward bend; bend at hips with knees bent; never fully fold
6Hold Feet / KidneysHigh blood pressureAvoid head-below-heart; limit to 45° forward bend maximum
6Hold Feet / KidneysOsteoporosisSkip full forward bend; substitute gentle waist rotation instead
7Clench FistsHand / wrist arthritisSoften grip; partial fist acceptable; stop before pain
7Clench FistsGlaucoma / eye conditionAvoid fiercely widening eyes; keep eyes soft and relaxed
8Bouncing FeetOsteoporosis — CRITICALSKIP heel drop entirely. Gentle heel raise + very slow controlled lowering. No impact at all.
8Bouncing FeetHip / knee replacementSkip all impact; gentle raise and lower only; or skip entirely
AllAll movementsFull wheelchair / mobility limitAll 8 adaptable to seated; emphasize upper body, breath, and mental intention
AllAll movementsPregnancy 2nd/3rd trimesterAvoid deep bends and extreme ROM; slow all movements; prioritize breath
