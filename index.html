The 8 Movements

Tap any movement to expand · YouTube links included

🧘 Opening Position · 開始
Feet together, step left to shoulder width. Hands to lower dantian (one on top of other). Close eyes. Breathe naturally for ~30 seconds to calm body and mind.
🌿 Closing Position · 結束
Inhale raising arms to each side with palms upward until just over your head. Exhale lowering with palms down to lower dantian. Repeat 3 cycles. Finish with hands on lower dantian, eyes closed, 30+ seconds. Open eyes, relax arms, return left foot.
